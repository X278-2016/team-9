This website is designed to help you if you owe money to a company.

What kinds of things might I owe money for?

  1. You might owe money to a company because you didn't pay back all your credit card bills.
  2. You might owe money because you had a car repossessed.
  3. You might owe money for medical bills.
  4. You might owe money becuase you bought something and didn't pay it all back.
  5. You might owe money for private student loans. This website only applies to private student loans. If you owe student loans you got from the federal government, you should visit https://studentaid.ed.gov/sa/repay-loans for more information.

You will be asked several questions. The questions will help us give you the best information for your situation. This website can give you information if:

  1. Bill collectors are calling you.
  2. You owe money and are worried about being sued.
  3. You have been sued and have to go to court.
  4. You have already been to court and lost.
This information cannot take the place of advice from a lawyer. Each case is different and needs individual legal advice.
